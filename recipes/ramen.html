Ramen

what is Ramen

is a Japanese noodle dish. It consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles (中華麺, chūkamen) served in a broth; common flavors are soy sauce and miso, with typical toppings including sliced pork (chāshū), nori (dried seaweed), menma (bamboo shoots), and scallions.

ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • (3 ounce) package any flavor ramen noodles
  • 1 slice American cheese

steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender, about 2 minutes.
  3. Pour out water, then stir in seasoning packet and cheese until well blended.
